About Me

Welcome! I am a 6th year PhD candidate in Astronomy at the University of Maryland, College Park.

My research is focused on time-domain and transient astronomy. I primarily search for, discover, and characterize tidal disruption events (TDEs) and their host galaxies at optical and ultraviolet wavelengths. I am also a member of the Zwicky Transient Facility (ZTF) collaboration TDE & AGN Science Working Group.

Starting in September 2024, I will be starting a postdoctoral scholar position at the University of California, Berkeley working in the TREx group.
